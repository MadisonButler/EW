Vanilla Butter Cake Recipe

Madison Butler
Cook time: 1 hr 10 min Serving Size: 3 8-inch cakes
Ingredients
  • Nonstick baking spray
  • 2/3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 cups buttermilk, at room temperature
  • 4 large eggs
  • 3 ounces vegetable oil
  • 1 tablespoon pure vanilla extract
  • 2 cups & 2 tablespoons granulated sugar
  • 2 cups cake flour
  • 2 1/4 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon table salt
  • 12 tablespoons unsalted butter, softened
Instructions

Step 1

Preheat your oven to 350 degrees & grease and lightly flour three round pans.

Step 2

Combine the buttermilk, eggs, vegetable oil, and vanilla in a bowl or a large measuring cup, and whisk until smooth.

Step 3

Combine the sugar, cake flour, all-purpose flour, baking powder, baking soda, and salt in the bowl of your stand up mixed, if you have one. Whisk to combine the dry ingredients.

Step 4

Place the mixed bowl on the mixer. With your mixer on low, begin adding chuncks of softened butter and continue blending until the mixture is sandy and you see no chuncks of butter remaining.

Step 5

Now add 1/4 of the liquid ingredients to the dry ingredients and blend on low to combine. Raise the mixer to medium-high speed and beat for 2 minutes, scraping the bowl halfway through beating. Add half of the remaining liquid ingredients to the bowl, blend on low to combine and then scrape the bowl. Add the remaining liquid ingredients and continue to blend on the lowest speed for 2 minutes.

Step 6

Divide the batter into the prepared pans and bake until a toothpick insterted into the center of the cakes come out with a few most crumbsstuck to it, 25 to 30 minutes.

Step 7

Cool the layers in their pans for 15 minutes, then turn them onto a cooling rack until cooled completely.
